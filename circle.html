Circle

Formulas:-


1] Area (A) = πr2
2] Circumference (C) = 2πr
3] Diameter (d) = 2r
4] Radius (r) = √(A/π)


Applications:-


1] Calculate distance :- Circles are used by GPS to determine distance. It locates points and determines their separations from the satellite using a circle theory.
2] Wheels :- Circles are used for wheels, allowing smooth movement and efficient transportation.
3] Clocks :- Circular clocks with rotating hands are commonly used for timekeeping.
4] Symbol for Infinity :- The circle represents infinity, symbolizing endlessness and continuity.
