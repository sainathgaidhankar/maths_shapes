Triangle

Formulas:-


1] Area (A) = (1/2)bxh
2] Perimeter (P) = a+b+c
3] Semi-perimeter (S) = (a+b+c)/2


Applications:-


1] Stability :-Triangles are strong and stable shapes. They are used in structures like bridges and roofs to provide support and prevent collapse.
2] Mathematics :- Triangles are fundamental in mathematics. They help calculate angles and side lengths, and are essential in fields like physics and engineering.
3] Warning Signs :- Triangles are often used on road signs to indicate caution or warning. An upright triangle can signal hazards ahead.
4] Balance in Design :- Triangles are used in visual arts and design to create balance and harmony. They can be arranged to guide the viewer's eye and add visual interest.
