Cube

Formulas:-


1] Volume (V) = a3
2] Surface area (A) = 6a2
3] Diagonal (d) = √3a
4] Edges (a) = V⅓


Applications:-


1] Building Blocks :- Cubes are used as building blocks for construction and toys, allowing for easy stacking and creating structures.
2] Storage Containers :- Cubes serve as efficient storage containers or boxes, making use of space and allowing for easy stacking.
3] Dice :- Cubes are used as dice in games and gambling, with each face marked with a different number for random outcomes.
4] 3D Modeling and Graphics :- Cubes are a basic shape in 3D modeling and graphics, often used as a starting point for creating more complex objects.
