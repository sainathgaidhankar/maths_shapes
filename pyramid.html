Prism

Formulas:-


1] Volume (V) = (Length x Hight x Width)/3
2] Surface Area (A) = Base Area + (0.5 x Perimeter of Base x Height)
3] Base Area = Length x Width
4] Perimeter of Base = 2 x (Length + Width)


Applications:-


1] Architectural Landmarks :- Pyramids are iconic structures found in ancient civilizations, serving as cultural landmarks and symbols of human achievement.
2] Unique Tea Bags :- Pyramid-shaped tea bags are designed to enhance the infusion and flavor of tea, providing a unique brewing experience.
3] Hierarchical Structures :- The pyramid shape is commonly used to represent hierarchical structures, illustrating levels of authority or leadership.
4] Mystical Symbolism :- Pyramids hold mystical and esoteric significance in various cultures, believed to possess energetic properties or used for meditation and spiritual practices.
