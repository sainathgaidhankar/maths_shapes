sphere

Formulas:-


1] Volume (V) = (4/3)πr3
2] Surface area (a) = 4πr2
3] Diameter (d) = 2r
4] Radious (r) = d/2


Applications:-


1] Sports Equipment :- Spheres are used for sports balls like soccer balls and basketballs, allowing for consistent and predictable movement.
2] Planets and Celestial Bodies :- Spheres represent planets and celestial bodies in our universe, mimicking their natural shape.
3] 3D Modeling and Graphics :- Spheres are commonly used in 3D modeling and computer graphics to create objects with smooth and uniform curvature.
4] Decorative Ornaments :- Spheres serve as decorative ornaments in sculptures, garden features, and holiday decorations.
